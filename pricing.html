Pay-as-you-go

Pay only when the platform is working for you.

The monthly fee covers platform access and every feature in your plan. The per-use pricing covers the underlying infrastructure on features that consume real resources. A slow February costs less than a peak-season August. That is by design.

Voice AI

A small per-minute voice engine cost plus token-based LLM usage. In practice, pennies per call.

Conversation AI

Token-based pricing. You pay only for the messages the AI actually generates.

SMS & MMS

Standard per-message carrier pass-through rates. Same for inbound and outbound.

Email

Standard per-send rate after the included monthly volume. Ample headroom for most shops.

Outbound call minutes

Standard per-minute carrier pass-through on your LC Phone or Twilio numbers.

Extra numbers & seats

Additional phone numbers and user seats above plan limits at flat monthly rates.
