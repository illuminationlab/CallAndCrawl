How the math works

The assumptions behind your number.

Missed calls. Pest problems are emotional and urgent. A homeowner who just saw a roach at 9pm is not going to leave you a voicemail — they are going to keep dialing until someone picks up. Most pest callers who hit voicemail never call back. Multiply your daily missed-call volume by your average ticket and that drop-off across 260 working days, and the number gets large fast.

Empty driveways and no-shows. When the truck rolls and nobody is home, you eat the drive time and the tech time. Automated appointment reminders (24h, 2h, 30min) plus a pre-service confirmation text cut that rate materially.

What the calculator does not yet include. Quarterly accounts drifting into silent-cancel, termite bonds not renewed because nobody followed up 60 days out, lapsed customers who forgot your name, and review-driven local-search losses. In other words: the number you see here is a floor, not a ceiling.
