AI Tools

The two agents that answer when you cannot.

This is where Call and Crawl departs from every other pest control CRM on the market. Most competitors bolt a ChatGPT-style widget on the website and call it AI. We ship two separate, production-grade AI agents — one for voice, one for text — and they share context, knowledge base, and calendar.

Voice AI

Your 24/7 AI receptionist.

Voice AI is a phone-based AI agent that answers inbound calls in a natural, human-sounding voice and takes real action during the call. It is not a menu tree. It is not a voicemail transcriber. It is a conversational agent with access to your live calendar, your CRM, your service menu, and your business rules.

  • Answers on the first ring with a greeting you script, using advanced speech recognition and natural language understanding.
  • Books the job by looking at your live calendar, suggesting open slots, and writing the appointment directly into your schedule.
  • Creates the CRM record mid-call — contact, opportunity, pest issue, urgency, notes.
  • Triggers workflows during or after the call — follow-up SMS, team notification, pipeline stage change, quote email.
  • Transfers to a human when the caller asks, or on high-intent signals you define: commercial account, termite inspection, wildlife emergency.
  • Sends the confirmation text automatically — appointment details, payment link, pre-service checklist, tech bio.
  • Makes outbound calls from a workflow trigger — follow up on a form fill in seconds, confirm tomorrow’s appointments, re-engage a lapsed quarterly.
  • 200+ voices, tunable tone, pace, language, personality. Voice AI chat widget embeds on your site — visitors speak live in the browser.
  • Post-call summaries and sentiment scores logged in the dashboard. Works on LC Phone and Twilio numbers.

Conversation AI

Instant answers on every written channel.

Conversation AI lives on your website chat widget and inside every messaging channel you have: SMS, email, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, WhatsApp, Google Business messages, live chat. It engages visitors and leads 24/7 with natural, human-like written responses.

  • Answers any question from a custom knowledge base you own and can edit anytime — documents, FAQs, a crawled version of your website, uploaded PDFs, service menus.
  • Qualifies leads by collecting name, contact info, pest issue, service interest, and urgency through natural conversation.
  • Books appointments directly in the chat flow without handing off.
  • Escalates to a human when the visitor asks, or on triggers you define.
  • Understands audio messages on mobile — visitors hold to talk, the AI extracts intent and details.
  • Maintains context across a conversation so it does not ask the same question twice.
  • Suggestive or fully automated mode, per channel — drafts for approval or handles end-to-end.
  • Shares the unified inbox with every other conversation — SMS, email, DMs, live chat — so nothing lives in a silo.
