About Call and Crawl

We build software for pest control.
Only pest control.

Call and Crawl is not a generic home-services CRM with pest control stamped on the landing page. It is not a dispatch tool that added a marketing module in a minor release. It is a full, modern, AI-powered platform built for one industry — and that industry is pest control.

Why we only serve pest control

Brilliant operators. Broken tools. A phone that would not stop ringing.

We started Call and Crawl because every pest control owner we talked to was running the same broken stack: a dated CRM from the 2000s, a separate texting app, a Mailchimp account nobody logged into, an answering service that forwarded voicemails they never listened to, and a yellow pad next to the phone for the calls that came in anyway. The techs were in the truck. The phone was ringing. The calls were being missed.

Generic home-services software did not fix that problem. It just gave shops a nicer-looking version of the same stack. The incumbents in pest software were too big and too slow to rebuild around AI. And nobody was talking to PCOs the way PCOs actually talk — about callbacks, swarm season, route density, termite bonds, quarterlies, and the ten-cent margin that route-day drive time eats.

So we built the thing we wished existed. Pest-control-first. AI-first. Affordable. White-labeled. Built by people who read Pest Control Technology and PMP Magazine the way other people read tech blogs.

Our mission

Help pest control businesses grow by giving them a platform that answers every call, nurtures every lead, keeps every recurring customer, and does not cost more than a good tech’s truck payment.

Full stop. That is the brief. Every product decision we make is measured against it.

The tagline

Infest Your Market. Dominate Your Territory.

It is a flip of the metaphor. In the field, pests are the enemy. In the market, the pest control business that shows up faster, responds better, and spreads into every zip code is the good infestation — the one that takes share from the sleepy shop down the road.

Call and Crawl is built to make your business that pest: fast, relentless, everywhere, inevitable.

What we believe

Four principles that shape every decision.

The phone is the business.

Pest control runs on inbound calls. Everything else — the CRM, the routing, the invoicing, the marketing — is downstream of whether somebody picked up. That is why Voice AI is core to the platform and not a premium upsell.

AI should do the work owners hate, not the work they love.

Owners love the customers, the techs, the treatment plans, the problem-solving. They hate the paperwork, the reminder calls, the follow-ups, the chasing invoices. We built the AI to take the second pile and leave the first.

Niche beats generic.

A tool built for one industry beats a tool that flexes to ten. You should not have to configure a “service business template” in a generic CRM. You should open the platform and see pipelines named after the way pest work moves: new lead, inspection scheduled, proposal sent, bond signed, quarterly active, renewal at risk.

Transparent pricing, no surprises.

A flat platform fee plus pay-for-what-you-use on the parts that cost money to run. No mystery invoices. No “enterprise pricing — contact sales.” No bundled modules you will never open.

The team

Small. Focused. Low overhead.

We are a small team of operators, engineers, and marketers who have built technology in the home-services and vertical-SaaS space before. We are backed by people who have operated service businesses themselves. We are headquartered wherever the work needs us. We keep our overhead low so our prices stay low.

We are not going to tell you about our office ping-pong table. We are going to tell you what we read this week that will make you better at your business. Subscribe to the newsletter if that sounds right.

What comes next

The most useful pest control platform on the market.

We are building the most useful pest control platform on the market. Every quarter, new features. Every month, new templates, workflows, and AI improvements — all informed by the shops actually running Call and Crawl in the field. If you want to be one of them, we would love to meet you.
